From Amazon, Intui-Shop and Recommendation Engines

Apr 3

The benefits of “Intui-Shop” go beyond not having to think about anything ever again. They include discovering, and buying, exciting new products that you haven’t even heard of yet, and catching up on old Amazon Wish List items that you haven’t thought about in years.

Let’s put it this way: You know how, when you order something on Amazon, you lose track of when it’s supposed to arrive? So that, when you find it at your door, it feels like your birthday? Now every day can—and will—be like that.

A humour piece from the New Yorker. I doubt we’ll actually see this – there are too many risqué and truly crap products on Amazon to full automate it entirely, and real AI is too damn hard. But the future of recommendation engines definitely can do down this path.
